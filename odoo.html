FAQ

Common questions about Odoo ERP.

How long does an Odoo implementation take?▾
It depends on the scope. A focused implementation (e.g. CRM + Sales + Accounting for a small team) can go live in 3–4 weeks. A full multi-module implementation for a larger team typically takes 6–12 weeks including data migration and training.
Can you migrate our data from our current system?▾
Yes. We migrate data from Excel, accounting software, other ERP systems and databases. We clean and validate the data before migration to ensure your Odoo system starts with accurate records.
Do you support UAE VAT compliance?▾
Yes. We configure Odoo for UAE VAT — including tax rules, VAT invoices, credit notes and the VAT return report format required by the Federal Tax Authority (FTA).
What happens after go-live?▾
We provide post-launch support, additional training sessions as your team grows, and ongoing customization as your business evolves. We stay involved as your long-term Odoo partner.
Can Odoo Community handle a serious business operation?▾
Yes, when it is scoped and implemented correctly. We focus on Community Edition for core ERP workflows such as CRM, sales, invoicing, purchasing, inventory, operations, reporting and custom process automation. If a requested feature is not available in Community by default, we explain the options clearly before development starts.
